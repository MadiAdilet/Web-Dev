Swiss Cheese Fondue

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredient Quantity
Gruy√®re cheese 200g
Emmental cheese 200g
White wine 1 cup
Garlic 1 clove
Kirsch (cherry brandy) 2 tbsp
Cornstarch 1 tsp

Steps:

1. Grate both cheeses and mix with cornstarch.

2. Rub fondue pot with garlic clove.

3. Heat wine in pot until simmering.

4. Gradually add cheese, stirring constantly.

5. Stir in kirsch and season with pepper and nutmeg.

6. Serve with bread cubes for dipping.

Swiss Raclette

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredient Quantity
Raclette cheese 500g
Baby potatoes 1kg
Pickled onions 1 jar
Cornichons (small pickles) 200g
Dried meat (optional) 200g

Steps:

1. Boil potatoes until tender.

2. Slice raclette cheese into individual portions.

3. Melt cheese under raclette grill or in small pans.

4. Serve melted cheese over hot potatoes.

5. Accompany with pickles, onions, and dried meat.
